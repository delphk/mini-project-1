Culture

Sumo

Sumo is a competitive full-contact wrestling sport which originated in Japan - the only country where it is practiced professionally. Enjoying sumo wrestling in Tokyo is a fantastic cultural experience whilst in Japan.

Manga and anime

Manga are comics created in Japan in the 19th century. Tokyo is a heaven for Japanese manga and anime fan because there are a lot of shops that specialize in selling manga and anime related products. Akihabara is the beating heart of the manga and anime movement. With its electronics shops, maid cafes and anime stores, it is a paradise for any self-proclaimed otaku.

Kimono

Kimono might be one of the first things everybody thinks of when hearing “Japanese culture.” The word "kimono" literally means a "thing to wear". Today, kimono are omost often worn by women on special occasions. Thus, actually wearing one while exploring Tokyo should be on the list of everyone coming to Japan!
