Attractions

Tokyo Tower

Tokyo Tower is actually the 13 meters taller than the Eiffel Tower. Although Tokyo Tower is taller than the Eiffel Tower, the advances in steel technology make it almost half its weight.

Sensō-ji

Sensō-ji is perhaps the most famous and photographed of Tokyo's temples. Completed in the year 645, the five-story pagoda is the oldest Buddhist temple in the capital.

Tsukiji Fish Market

Tsukiji is the world’s busiest seafood market. The charm of going to the market can not be known better than jetlagged tourists with pre-dawn hours to fill especially to catch the 5 a.m. tuna auction.
